The School

Madhusudan Public School (MPS) has been started from 1st April 2009 to cater quality English education in order to meet the needs of the fast changing 21st century world.

The school is affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi. The Affiliation No. is 3430268 and School No. is 66462 .

Madhusudan Public School popularly known as MPS which has come a long way in nurturing young minds since its inception. Its impressive jouney started in 2009 with the ambience for a ground breaking education system. The school provides various facilities like VAST PLAYGROUND, DIGITAL CLASS, COMPUTER LAB, SCIENCE LAB and other separrate LAB for various subjects, spacious and well staked LIBRARY. School follows 100 percent child-centered approach with emphasis on Continuous & Comprehensive Evaluation system implemented by C.B.S.E. Participation in the co-scholastic activities has range from YOGA, KARATE, ART & CARFT, MUSIC, DANCE etc. are an integral part of the learning system.

The school is a co-educational English medium and strictly follows CCE guidelines provided by CBSE, New Delhi. Our School holds a motto - "MOTIVATE PERSONAL STRENGTH " to provide the students all sorts of opportunities for achieving all-round and harmonious development.

MPS is situated at Asantalia 4 kms away from Railway Station Chakradharpur in west direction linked withChakradharpur - Ranchi Main Road NH-75 (E). It is nearly 5 minutes drive to reach MPS which is nearby SDO Office, Porahat, Chakradharpur, W. Singhbhum, Jharkhand.
